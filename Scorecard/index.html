Carbon Scorecard

What's a carbon scorecard?

 

We track the estimated emissions from our smart contracts and associated wallets. At the moment we are using https://carbon.fyi/ to obtain an estimate of the C02 emissions associated with our on-chain interactions.

 

We are also looking at getting an estimate of the C02 associated with our hosting and other equipment use. This is likely to be minimal, so in the meantime we will just buy waaaay more credits that we need, so we can be sure of being carbon negative!
